Have You Seen

You don't need to wonder when the experiences are right here. I would still encourage you to stay in touch with how our global neighbors are doing.

These posters were a smaller project, but one I think is important.

I wanted to help others realize that the problem was not a whole demographic, but a subset of specific people. Understanding and empathy goes beyond one's country borders. With so much availability to first-hand experiences, I wanted to share a few videos from citizens' whose voices deserved to be heard.

My intention is not to capitalize on anything, but to remind others to avoid prejudice or discrimination.
