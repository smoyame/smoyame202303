Have You Seen

You don't need to wonder when the experiences are right here. I would still encourage you to stay in touch with how our global neighbors are doing.

Like many others, I couldn't help but wonder how Ukrainians and surrounding residents were faring as war began. In an age where we have such quick access to first-hand accounts, it was interesting to see people focus on headlines instead of people. I wanted to know how some folks were doing and if they were okay. Then, I wanted to make sure I tried to share their stories with whoever I could. What about those who didn't agree to their leader's actions but had no choice? How were they?
