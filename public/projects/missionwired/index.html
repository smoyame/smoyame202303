MissionWired

I've gone beyond my initial role and helped improve production strategy by picking up new CRMs and propietary programming languages when I see a need.

At MissionWired, I've helped bring campaigns for clients such as Make-A-Wish, Raphael Warnock, Save the Children, the Democratic Governors Association, Guiding Eyes for the Blind, democracy FIRST, and many more!

I've adapted to and refined production processes in CRMs such as HubSpot, MailChimp, NGP, Action Network, and Acoustic. As each client requests increasingly investigative A/B tests to encourage engagement with their mission, I dig in and learn how each platform works and what I can use to help keep the focus on the action these clients are trying to carry out in our communities.

Beyond CRMs, I also continuously strive to improve any existing materials in new and old internal production tools. From rapid response campaigns to welcome stream graphics, there's no consideration too small.
